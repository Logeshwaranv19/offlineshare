The OffgridShare Ecosystem

💾

Offline-First

Powered by PouchDB and CouchDB. Your data stays on your device and syncs automatically when a local peer is detected.

⚡

True P2P Transfer

Direct WebRTC pipes between devices. No middleman, no servers—just pure, high-speed data flow over LAN/Wi-Fi.

📱

Universal Support

A single ecosystem for Android (Capacitor), Desktop (Electron), and Web (PWA). Share across all your devices seamlessly.

🛡️

Dual Storage

The Vault for metadata and small files, and Quick Share for streaming gigabytes of data without memory limits.

🔐

Absolute Privacy

No accounts, no tracking, and no cloud servers. Your data never leaves your local network infrastructure.

🤳

QR Pairing

Instantly pair devices using QR codes or Signal Strings. Simple, secure, and completely offline connection setup.

Built for Resilience

The Dual-Storage Strategy

OffgridShare intelligently separates data to ensure maximum performance and reliability.

📂 The Vault

A persistent local database (PouchDB) for your metadata, messages, and small files. It stays in sync across your devices via CouchDB whenever they meet on a local network.

🚀 Quick Share

A high-performance P2P streaming pipe for large files. Send 4K movies or large archives directly from device to device without consuming internal database storage.
